Where Local Farming Meets Lasting Support.

Brandwood Ag Services is a locally owned company proudly based in Gilroy, California. What began as a small operation driven by a passion for agriculture has grown into a trusted partner for farms across the region. We specialize in solutions that support soil health, increase yields, and help family farms thrive for generations. Our team understands the land because we live on it — and we’re here to serve those who do the same.
